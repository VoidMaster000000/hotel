About Us

Your Comfortable & Down-to-Earth Stay

Pakeeza One Hotel is a warm, welcoming establishment nestled in the vibrant heart of Johar Town, Lahore. Situated directly opposite a beautiful park, our hotel offers a peaceful retreat with lovely garden views — a rare find in the bustling city.


Surrounded by excellent local restaurants and just minutes away from the Lahore Expo Center, Emporium Mall, and Gaddafi Stadium, we are the ideal choice for families, business travelers, and visitors attending trade fairs. Whether you're here for work or leisure, we promise a stay that feels just like home.
